Changua

Description

Milk soup with eggs

A mixture of equal amounts of water and milk is heated with a dash of salt.

Changua typical breakfast of Colombia

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Water
  • Eggs
  • Cilantro
  • Stale bread

Steps

  1. Heat the milk and the water together
  2. Add the eggs without breaking the yolk
  3. Garnish with curly cilantro
  4. Add pieces of stale bread
